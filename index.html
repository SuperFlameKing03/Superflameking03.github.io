Elebud
Elebud used to roam the jungles in the southern part of the region, but have recently become more of a rarity. Their inner power comes from the forests they roam.
Coalty
Coalty are strong, yet hot headed. The red patches of fur on its body are always exiting heat, waiting for the perfect moment to spark a flame.
Iguopa
Iguopa are as quick as a river current. The hard plating on its head and back were eroded into the perfect shape and act as an armor plating.
Freechick
Freechick are only barely learning how to fly. Most of the time though they simply sit around rather dazed and rely on their parents to provide food. They do have a sense of adventure though, and a yearning for freedom.
Wittybitty
On their own, Wittybitty aren't really a threat at all. It's when a bunch of them gather together that you should become very afraid. They are stronger in groups and take on an intimidating look to scare off predators.
Moosicle
Moosicle are very young and often stick close to their parents. Seeing the raw strength of their parents are often a driving force for them to grow up and become stronger. Thanks to this determination, they are typically only with their parents for about a year after their birth

Team Dawn are one side to the coin of creating a new nation. Mostly comprised of newcomers, Team Dawn strives for the progression of the region and to reach new hights, ones that can only come by letting go of our past. They believe in and strive for a progressive economoy unlike the likes of which we have ever seen! The most radical among them even claim that our history halts our progression and the only way to move forward is to leave it all behind.

Team Dusk are one side to the coin of creating a new nation. Mostly comprised of natives, Team Dusk strives for the preservation of their lands and traditions, wonderful and effective ways of life that our ancestors have discovoered for us. They believe in and strive for a wider spread version of their ways of life in order to share this beautiful system with all! The most radical of them even claim that the only way forward is to focus on what we have and stay away from anything that even risks otherwise.

A long, long time ago in a far distant land a great terror fell from the sky. Retaliating in fear, the people fled from their land in search for a new home, protected by a legendary beast from their homeland. They eventually found a new land to settle in, but little did these newcomers realize that they were not the first to try and settle here. At first they tried to make peace, but could not get along, as the ideals of the natives were very different from the ideals of the newcomers. Upon witnessing the ancient guardian of these lands, the newcomers began to fear a repeat of the events back in their homeland known as "The Darkest Day." Eventually, tensions reached an all time high and the two legendary guardians clashed for territory. This dragged the newcomers and the natives into the conflict, which became all out war. When all seemed lost for both sides, a White Pikachu decended from the heavens and sealed away both legendary beasts. It's been 100 Years since these events have taken place, and we are only now truly beginning to recover. The newcomers and natives nowadays are striving even more than ever before for unity and peace. But no one knows if the tales about these legendary guardians or even a White Pikahcu are true or exagerated belief.
